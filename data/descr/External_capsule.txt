<h4>External Capsule</h4></hr>
A series of white matter fiber tracts in the brain. These fibers run between the putamen and the claustrum. They contains fibers known as corticocortical association fibers,responsible for connecting the cerebral cortex to another cortical area.  The external capsule is a route for cholinergic fibers from the basal forebrain to the cerebral cortex.</hr>
