<h4>CSF - Cerebrospinal fluid</h4></hr>
A clear, colorless body fluid found in the brain and spinal cord. CSF acts as a cushion or buffer for the brain, providing basic mechanical and immunological protection to the brain inside the skull. CSF also serves a vital function in cerebral autoregulation of cerebral blood flow. CSF occupies the subarachnoid space and the ventricular system around and inside the brain and spinal cord. </hr>
