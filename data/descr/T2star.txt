<h4>T2star - effective T2</h4></hr>
Used to detect deoxygenated hemoglobin, methemoglobin, or hemosiderin in lesions and tissues. Diseases with such patterns include intracranial hemorrhage, arteriovenous malformation, cavernoma, hemorrhage in a tumor, punctate hemorrhages in diffuse axonal injury, superficial siderosis, thrombosed aneurysm, phleboliths in vascular lesions, and some forms of calcification.</hr>
